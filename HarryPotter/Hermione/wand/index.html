Hermione Granger's wand

Hermione performed powerful magic with this wand during her time at Hogwarts. She was able to produce a corporeal Patronus, an otter, at the age of fifteen, a very advanced piece of magic and proof that she possessed superior magical ability.[2] Due to Hermione's talent and hard work she was top of her class in every subject, except Defence Against the Dark Arts where she came second to Harry Potter.
